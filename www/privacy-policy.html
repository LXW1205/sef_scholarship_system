Privacy Policy

1 Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you register for an account, apply for scholarships, or communicate with us. This may include your name, email address, student ID, academic records, and family income information.

2 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and evaluate your scholarship applications.
  • Communicate with you about your account and applications.
  • Improve our services and development.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3 Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

4 Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties except to provide our services, such as sharing application details with scholarship reviewers and committee members.

5 Updates to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
